About

I've spent 25 years in the rooms where policy meets delivery — building the frameworks, oversight models, and performance architectures that help governments turn intent into real outcomes. My work sits at the intersection of public policy, service design, and digital governance, and I've had the privilege of doing it across some of Canada's most complex multi-organization environments.

Working Together

I work with senior leaders who need a trusted, experienced hand — someone who understands how governments actually function and can help navigate the gap between where things are and where they need to be. Whether that means designing a new governance model, untangling a fragmented performance system, or advising on a complex strategic challenge, I bring rigour, judgment, and practical focus to every engagement.
